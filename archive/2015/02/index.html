GC Digital Research Bootcamp

Learn to use digital tools to jumpstart your research.

Four Days of Hands-On Workshops

The GC Digital Research Bootcamp is a four day workshop where you will learn how to use a range of tools and methods for doing digital research. There are multiple tracks targeted at different skillsets and disciplinary focuses.
